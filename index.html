What is MoodWave?

MoodWave reads your room in real time using temperature, humidity, noise, and light sensors. It then picks music based on those readings to help increase productivity, lower stress, and support creativity throughout the day.

The Science + How MoodWave Works

Your environment affects your body and attention. Heat, humidity, brightness, and noise can change stress, energy, and focus. MoodWave watches those signals and adjusts the music response to keep the room in a better range.

  1. The Arduino board reads temperature, humidity, noise, and light in real time.
  2. Python cleans the stream and computes live metrics.
  3. The dashboard updates instantly in the browser.
  4. MoodWave tags the room state based on the current conditions.
  5. The music system picks a fitting track for your goal and context.

Why Does It Matter?

Most people try to force focus while the room works against them. MoodWave helps by responding to the room first, then shaping sound to support what you are trying to do. It is a practical way to make your space feel more usable without changing hardware every time.
